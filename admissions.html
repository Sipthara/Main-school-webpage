Admissions

Admission procedure

  Our intake is in the month of January each year. A prospective parent should make it to the school office and fill the initial inquiry form or email a pdf after filling in the application form attached below to the school. After, which a public relations officer will brief the parent about the applicable fees structure and assist the parent with scheduling a placement test to evaluate the child's eligibility for entrance. The child will be tested in English & Maths from the year 1 level up to year 5. This placement test process is applicable to all grades in the school.

  After the evaluation is complete the principal will assess the paper and make a decision with regard to admission to the relevant grade. Then the parents will have an interview with the Head of Administration. Please note that apart from the above-mentioned criteria selection is solely based on the collective decision by the management of Sipthara.
